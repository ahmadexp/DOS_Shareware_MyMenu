
CONVERT.EXE converts PROCOMM+ 1.x, QMODEM 4.2x or TELIX 3.xx phone
directory to TELEMATE phone directory

Usage: CONVERT /P <PROCOMM+ phone directory> <TELEMATE phone directory>
       CONVERT /Q <QMODEM phone directory> <TELEMATE phone directory>
       CONVERT /T <TELIX phone directory> <TELEMATE phone directory>

 e.g.  CONVERT /P PCPLUS.DIR TM.FON
       CONVERT /Q QMODEM.FON TM.FON
       CONVERT /T TELIX.FON TM.FON
