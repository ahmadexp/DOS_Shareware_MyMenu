
7BITDOC.EXE is a utility that convert the 8 bit documents to 7 bit
printable documents.

Usage:
    C> 7BITDOC

It will convert the following files automatically.

    TELEAMTE.TXT  ->  TELEMATE.PRN
    TMSCRIPT.TXT  ->  TMSCRIPT.PRN
    TMSTAT.TXT    ->  TMSTAT.PRN
    TMHOST.TXT    ->  TMHOST.PRN
