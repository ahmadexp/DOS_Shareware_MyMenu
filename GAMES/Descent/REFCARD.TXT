DEMO PLAYBACK CONTROLS
----------------------

UP ARROW        play
DOWN ARROW      stop
RIGHT ARROW     single frame forward
LEFT ARROW      single frame reverse

SHIFT-RIGHT     fast forward
SHIFT-LEFT      fast reverse

CTRL-RIGHT      go to end
CTRL-LEFT       go to start


AUTOMAP
-------

Your ship's Automap will show areas of the mine you have seen.
Arrow keys will rotate the view left/right and up/down.  Hitting +/-
will zoom in/out.  Holding down 'S' will allow you to slide the
point of view around.
